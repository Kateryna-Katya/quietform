Still your hands.
Solve the shape.

Quiet Form's basically a puzzle game that makes you slow down — you drag pieces around, but if you rush it, they won't cooperate.

How It Works

  • 1

    Enter a Level

    Each stage presents a scattered arrangement of geometric forms waiting to be composed into a single, balanced shape.

  • 2

    Move With Intention

    Slow, deliberate drags guide pieces smoothly into place — rush them and they resist, adding tension to your meter.

  • 3

    Reach Stillness

    Once all the pieces click into place and your tension meter goes down, you've beaten the level.

Features

  • Slowness as Skill

    Unlike most games that want you to go fast, this one actually scores you on how patient you are.

  • Tactile Paper Physics

    Pieces carry just enough weight to feel real, settling with a soft resistance that makes every placement satisfying.

  • Negative Space Puzzles

    The trickier levels make you think about the empty spaces too, not just where the pieces go.

  • No Timers, No Pressure

    No timers, no game over screens — just you figuring out how everything fits together.
